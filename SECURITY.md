# Security Policy

## Restricted Data

Do not commit:

- Raw formulas
- Exact ratios
- Dosing details
- Private KYC
- Passport, SSN, driver license, or identity documents
- Patient information
- Medical records
- Private contracts
- Bank documents
- Unfiled patent materials
- Confidential investor documents

## Proof Boundary

Public systems may store:

- SHA-256 hashes
- Merkle roots
- Public IPFS CIDs
- Encrypted IPFS CIDs
- Anchor payload metadata
- Review status
- Visibility level
- Protocol event IDs

Private material must remain encrypted or stored outside the public repo.

## Medical / Legal / Securities Boundary

This repository does not provide:

- Medical advice
- Clinical validation
- FDA clearance
- Legal advice
- Securities advice
- Public token-sale infrastructure

All claims, RWA models, licenses, and offering-related materials require qualified legal, medical, regulatory, and securities review.
