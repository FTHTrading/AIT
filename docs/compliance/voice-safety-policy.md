# Voice Safety Policy

The voice layer is restricted to public-safe narration.

## Prohibited Narration Content

- Private KYC or identity documents
- Formula details, exact ratios, dosing specifics
- Patient records or raw medical files
- Private contracts and confidential investor files
- Investment solicitation language
- Unsupported medical certainty claims

## Mandatory Guardrails

- Medical pages include medical caution disclaimer.
- RWA and investor pages include legal-review disclaimer.
- Admin pages narrate metadata only.
- Admin voice mode remains disabled by default.

## Policy Statement

Voice functionality does not provide medical advice, legal advice, investment advice, or restricted document access.
