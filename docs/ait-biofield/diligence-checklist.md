# AIT Diligence Checklist

## Clinical and Regulatory
- IP ownership and assignment chain
- Patent and filing status
- Clinical data package
- FDA/regulatory classification
- IRB and trial status
- Treatment protocol versioning
- Safety and adverse event data

## Legal and Commercial
- Insurance and liability posture
- Medical advisory board composition
- License model terms
- Revenue model assumptions
- SPV/legal wrapper
- Securities review

## Data and Proof
- HIPAA/privacy review
- Claims review and source quality
- On-chain proof readiness and anchor runbook
