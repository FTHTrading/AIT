# AIT Biofield System Overview

## Positioning
AIT Biofield OS is not the therapy itself. It is the digital infrastructure layer around the therapy: evidence, documents, claims, licensing, capital formation, protocol registration, and on-chain proof.

## Scope
- Technology overview
- Use-case mapping
- Evidence and document registry
- Protocol registry
- Treatment-center licensing model
- Site/readiness scoring
- Clinical and regulatory diligence tracking
- IP and licensing proof
- RWA and revenue-rights structuring support
- On-chain anchoring
- Investor/funder document vault
- Compliance review workflow

## Safety and Compliance
- Company-provided claims require independent clinical and regulatory validation.
- No patient records or private medical data are stored on-chain.
- KYC/private diligence data remains restricted and non-public.
- Not medical advice. Not investment advice. Not an offer to sell securities.
